What is Cult'Movies?

Cult'Movies consists in screening all the greatest classic films of the movie industry. Discover these classics in movie theaters near your home. These special screenings take place 2 to 3 times a week and each week will be dedicated to a major director. Cult'Movies is internationally established: Paris, New York, London and Madrid.
